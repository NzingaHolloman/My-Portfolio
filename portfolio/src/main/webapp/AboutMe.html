The Journey to Computer Science

I was introduced to Computer Science during my first semester at West Chester University of Pennsylvania in fall 2018. At the beginning of my first computer science course, I had no idea what was going on. Later when I took the time to start programming on my own there was an instant connection. After spending hours working on a code and finally getting it running, I get this amazing feeling that fueled my curiosity and left a sense of pride.

Although I did develop my love for programming at West Chester University, the school it did not provide me with the tools I needed at the rate I needed them. By the start of the second semester, I knew I needed more opportunities, so I decided to transfer. Now I am surrounded by dedicated students open to new ideas at the luxurious North Carolina A&T State University. Can I get an AGGIE PRIDE!?
