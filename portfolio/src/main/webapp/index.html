Hi! Welcome to my portfolio.

I am a Computer Science student at the illustrious North Carolina A&T State University.

I started my computer science journey at West Chester University of Pennsylvania. At the beginning of my first computer Science course, I honestly had no idea what was going on, but later when I took the time to start programming on my own there was an instant connection. After spending hours working on a code and finally getting it running, I get would get this amazing feeling, that fueled my curiosity and left a sense of pride. As the school year progressed, saw the impact that technology has on the world, and the ability it has to change it for the better. Since then I have transferred to the #1 producer of African American undergraduates in Engineering, North Carolina A&T State University for their Computer Science program. Currenty I am being challenged, enlightened, and prepared for success.
